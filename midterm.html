Formatting and validating

What is the value of formatting your code?

Readibility. Clarity of code. Easier for others to understand what's happening in your code. See what's needed. Find mistakes/debug.

Why is it especially important to have clean, readable CSS code?

Cascading stylesheet. Styles written at the top can be overwritten by styles at the bottom. Specificity helps with this.

What is a validator? Why do you use it?

Checks your code, makes sure there are no issues. Helps with debugging.
